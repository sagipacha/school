Emily Thorne

Revenge 2011

Emily Thorne arrives in the Hamptons and rents a beach house near the Grayson family estate for the summer.
Emily's real name is actually Amanda Clarke, whose father, David Clarke, was convicted of treason when she was little.
After a long trial, he was sentenced to life imprisonment, and was murdered in prison by the people who framed him.
Amanda was separated from him after his trial and has not seen him since.
Now, she's back in the Hamptons as a young woman in her late 20s to get revenge on whoever hurt her and her father.
Her main target is Victoria Grayson, the illustrious mother of the Grayson family, who had an affair with David and then turned him in to the police.
